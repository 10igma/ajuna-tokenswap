Allowlist gate

While the gate is ON, only the owner() and explicitly-allowlisted addresses can deposit. withdraw is never gated. Per Phase 11 the multisig (via the timelock) flips this off to open to the public.

Pause & rescue

pause() halts both deposit and withdraw. Reserved for incident response. rescueToken recovers ERC20s mistakenly sent to the wrapper. The contract blocks rescuing the AJUN foreign asset and the wAJUN token itself, so the 1:1 backing cannot be broken.
